Every Bootstrap 5.3 component rendered with the project's brand tokens flowing through the --bs-* CSS-variable bridge. Toggle light/dark to verify the matrix. Anything that's not the brand purple in .btn-primary / .alert-primary / .text-bg-primary means the bridge is broken.
