How does Frollo connect to my bank?
We use the Consumer Data Right (CDR) for live, read-only access to your accounts. Your credentials never leave your bank.
How is my data protected?
All transmissions are TLS-encrypted; tokens are rotated; no access ever leaves Australia.
Can I revoke access?
Yes — disconnect at any time from your bank's dashboard or from Frollo Settings.
